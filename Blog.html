Affects of technology on our daily lives

There have been many positive as well as negative changes and affects in our life due to technology.

Some of the positive and negative affects of technology are:

Improvement in communication

The advancements in technology have led to the development of numerous means of electronic communication such as social networking websites, emails, etc. These advancements have helped us remove the inefficiency of time and distance in communication.

Although such technology has helped us stay closer to people far away from us, we also need to find a balance between our online life and real life. If we get overly attached to our social life online, it will be very harmful to our social skills in reality. Spending quality time with our friends and family is also important.

Changes in health industry

Technology has helped in the improvements of healthcare services. The majority of hospitals today are using modern technologies. Such advancements have made it easier to monitor your weight, heart rate and other health conditions at any time of the day with ease.

While such technologies have helped people a lot, there have also been many cases where people had been misguided by unprofessional information which they found on the Internet.

Convenience of Travelling

Modern transportation has made it easier for people to travel long distances. Technology has made it possible for our regular working t be more efficient and quicker.

Today we cannot imagine a life without such modern technologies in our day-to-day life as they have made our lives much easier and simpler. With this, the dependency of humans on such technologies is also increasing day-by-day.
